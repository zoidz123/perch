Verdict

Exploratory charts should make evidence-led decisions in 15 seconds

Recommended One screen

Findings

  • Long charts hide the decision.
  • Background repeats what the boss already knows.
  • Evidence dumps make phone review impractical.
  • Analogies dilute concrete findings.

Evidence

  • Phone review works when the verdict and proof fit on one screen.
  • Short linked proof survives annotation better than pasted source material.
Recommendation: approve only when the whole chart fits on one screen.
